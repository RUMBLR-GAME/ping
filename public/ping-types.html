How scoring works

Every ping is scored 0-10 across 5 dimensions by Claude AI. Only pings scoring above 4.0 make it to your feed.

Freshness
/2

How recent is the trigger? Newer = higher score. Signals older than 48h score below 1.0.

Budget
/2

How likely is there freelance budget? Funding rounds and active hiring score highest.

Skill match
/2

How closely does this match your profile? Direct matches score 2.0, adjacent skills 1.5.

Urgency
/2

How time-sensitive? No website + new registration = maximum urgency.

Reachability
/2

How accessible is the decision-maker? Email + LinkedIn = 2.0, single channel = 1.0-1.5.
