What is a "ping"?

A ping is a buying signal — an event that indicates a company is likely to need freelance services. We detect 6 types:

🏢 New registration

New ABN/business registered with no website. Needs brand + web immediately.

🌐 Domain purchased

Domain bought with no live site. Launching soon, needs design.

💰 Funding round

Seed or Series A closed. Budget unlocked for creative work.

👋 Leadership change

CMO/CTO departure. Interim creative support needed urgently.

📋 Hiring creative

Job posted for designer/developer. Often open to freelance too.

🔄 Tech migration

Switching platforms or rebranding. Needs specialist help.

Scoring rubric

Every ping receives a composite score from 0 to 10, calculated across 5 dimensions. Each dimension is scored 0-2 by Claude Sonnet, then normalised to a 10-point scale.

Signal freshness
/ 2

How recent is the buying trigger? Within 6 hours = 2.0, within 24h = 1.5, within 48h = 1.0, older = 0.5

Budget likelihood
/ 2

How likely is there budget? Funding round = 2.0, active hiring = 1.5, new registration = 1.0, domain only = 0.5

Skill match
/ 2

How closely does this match your profile? Direct match = 2.0, adjacent skill = 1.5, tangential = 1.0

Urgency
/ 2

How time-sensitive? Active hiring + no website = 2.0, leadership departure = 1.5, funding = 1.0

Reachability
/ 2

How accessible is the decision-maker? Email + LinkedIn = 2.0, LinkedIn only = 1.5, Instagram only = 1.0

Score interpretation

8.0 — 10.0

High confidence. Act immediately. These pings have strong signals, clear budget indicators, and accessible contacts.

5.0 — 7.9

Moderate confidence. Worth investigating. Signal is real but may need more research before outreach.

Below 5.0

Low confidence. Auto-filtered from your feed. Only shown if no higher-quality pings are available.

Data quality

Raw signals go through 3 quality gates before becoming a ping:

  • Validation gate — is the data complete and well-formed? Company name, location, and at least one signal indicator must be present.
  • Relevance gate — does this company operate in an industry where freelance design/dev/marketing services are typically needed?
  • Score gate — does the AI-computed score exceed 4.0/10? Below this threshold, the signal-to-noise ratio is too low to be useful.

AI-crafted messages

Every ping comes with 5 pre-written outreach messages, each taking a different strategic approach. All messages are personalised to the specific company, contact, and your skill profile.

The 5 message strategies

🎯 Direct & confident

Bold opener, lead with credentials and speed. Best for urgent pings (departures, no-website signals).

💛 Warm & personal

Human connection, shared interests. Best for founders and small companies where rapport matters.

📊 Value-led opener

Lead with free insight before asking. Best for skeptical or senior contacts who get pitched often.

🖼️ Portfolio-forward

Show, don't tell. Reference specific similar work. Best when you have directly relevant case studies.

⚡ Short & sweet

2-3 sentences, no pressure. Best for busy executives or when you're unsure of the opportunity quality.

Message quality

Every message is generated by Claude Sonnet and follows these principles:

  • Personalised to the company, not generic templates
  • References the specific buying signal (funding, registration, departure)
  • Keeps to 2-3 sentences — respects the recipient's time
  • Never uses salesy language, false urgency, or manipulation
  • Adapts tone to match the contact's communication style
