05 — Logo

Logo usage

The Ping mark is a stylised arrow loop — representing the cycle of opportunity discovery. It always appears as a single-colour mark, never outlined or modified.

White on Coral
Black on White
White on Black
Coral on Black

Clear space

Minimum clear space around the logo equals the height of the arrow loop portion of the mark. Never crowd the logo with text or other elements.

Don'ts

Never rotate the logo. Never apply drop shadows. Never use colours outside the approved palette. Never stretch or distort. Never place on busy photography without a solid background.

07 — Voice & tone

How Ping speaks

Ping is a sharp, confident friend who happens to know everyone in business. Not a corporate tool. Not a pushy salesperson. A trusted insider.

Voice attributes

🎯
Direct

No fluff. Lead with the value. Every word earns its place.

🤝
Warm

Friendly but not casual. Supportive but not soft. Peer-to-peer.

⚡
Urgent

Pings are time-sensitive. The language creates healthy FOMO without manipulation.
