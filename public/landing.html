    Frequently asked questions

    Last updated: March 2026

    What is Ping?

    Ping is a lead intelligence platform for freelancers and agencies. It surfaces real buying pings — like new business registrations, funding rounds, key hires, and departures — matched to your specific skills, and delivers them to you before anyone else.

    How does it work?

    You set up a skill profile (e.g., "UI/UX designer" or "brand strategist"). Ping's AI scans public data sources every day, matches relevant pings to your skills, and serves them as swipeable cards — each with the decision-maker's contact and an AI-crafted intro message.

    Where does the data come from?

    All pings are derived from publicly available sources: business registration databases (like OpenCorporates), new domain registrations via WHOIS, job posting aggregators, funding announcement databases, and public company filings. We never scrape private data.

    Is this legal?

    Yes. All data sources are public. Ping does not access private information or violate any data protection regulations. However, you are responsible for complying with outreach laws in your jurisdiction (e.g., anti-spam legislation).

    How much does it cost?

    The Free tier gives you 10 pings per day with AI intros — forever. Pro ($4.95/month) unlocks unlimited leads, Unlimited skill profiles, priority pings, and a built-in CRM. Team ($29/month) adds 5 seats and an agency dashboard.

    Can I cancel anytime?

    Yes. Cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

    How accurate are the leads?

    Ping matching is performed by AI and is generally high-quality, but not perfect. We recommend verifying contact details before reaching out. Ping is a discovery tool — it surfaces opportunities, but the relationship is yours to build.

    What skills can I target?

    Any professional skill: design, development, copywriting, marketing, consulting, photography, video production, accounting, legal services, and more. The AI matches pings to your specific expertise.

    Is there an API?

    API access is available on the Team plan. Documentation will be published at launch.

    When does Ping launch?

    Ping is currently in private beta. Register for beta to be first in line when we launch in your city.

    Documentation

    Last updated: March 2026

    Getting started

    Sign up for a free account, then set up your first skill profile. Ping will begin matching buying pings to your skills within 24 hours.

    Skill profiles

    A skill profile tells Ping what kind of work you do. Be specific: "Shopify e-commerce design" will yield more relevant pings than "web design". Free users get 1 profile; Pro users get 3.

    Understanding pings

    Ping tracks six types of pings:

    • New registrations — businesses registered with no website yet.
    • Domain purchases — domains bought but not yet live.
    • Funding rounds — companies that just raised money and are likely hiring.
    • Key departures — CMOs, creative directors, or CTOs leaving (creating interim needs).
    • Hiring pings — companies posting creative/technical roles (indicating budget).
    • Tech migrations — companies switching platforms (e.g., Squarespace to Shopify).

    Swipe interface

    Each ping appears as a card. Swipe right to "ping" the lead — this copies the AI-crafted intro to your clipboard. Swipe left to skip. Saved leads (Pro) appear in your CRM.

    AI intros

    Every card includes an AI-written outreach message tailored to the specific ping. Messages reference the buying trigger (e.g., "Congratulations on the funding round...") and your relevant skills. You can customise your AI voice in Pro settings.

    CRM (Pro)

    Saved leads are organised in a simple CRM with status tracking: Pinged, Replied, Meeting, Won, Lost. Filter by date, ping type, or status.

    Team features

    Team plans include 5 seats, a shared lead pool (no duplicate outreach), an agency dashboard, and API access for custom integrations.
